A little background

An accountant turned into a developer.

After a couple of year working as an external auditor at EY, I started to get bored of doing the exact same process over and over again. On the first weeks of the pandemic, I had a lot of free time after work so I started a couse in Udemy about BI and how to automate repetitive processes. This course tought about data analytics with Tableau, statistics algorithms and a little bit of programming with Python. This last one felt scarce, I wanted more. After that I took a 20hr course on Python and still wanted more on programming. That's how it began, Python brought me to JavaScript and so on.
My latest course was the Full Stack Developer Bootcamp at Plataforma 5. An +800hr course on JavaScript fundamentals, ReactJs, NodeJs, Express and PostgreSQL, but the most important of all, human skills. Starting everyday at 09:30 to 19:00, Monday to Friday for over 3 months. From working individually, to couples of 2, to groups of up to 6 people, working on different projects with Agile Methodologies.

A Few Accomplishments

Over the last few months I was able to work and contribute on different projects, individually and with a team of 5, such as:

The Movie Database

A web app to get to know the latest realeses on Movies and Tv Series and where to watch them according to your location. The app was built on ReactJs, with Redux for state management and ChackraUI for its design. For its Back End, it was developed with NodeJs, Express, Sequelize, PostgreSQL and JWT for session management.

SemiYa

An e-commerce that started as a Bootcamp project with a team of five and continued to work on it with Tomas Pinto Kramer. Started as a 2 week project but we wanted to polish it deploy it, still a work in progress. It has the same principles as the TMDB but it's designed with Tailwind UI.
Link to the E-Commerce Repository

HR Mobile App

Month long group project brought to us by Global News Group as a professional practice for the Coding Bootcamp. It's main goal is to create working teams across LatAm and monitoring its employees, letting them ask for any licenses so that his/her boss gets notified and approves or rejects it. Built in React Native with Expo and Redux for its Front and NodeJs, Express, Sequelize, PostgreSQL and JWT for session management for its Back.
